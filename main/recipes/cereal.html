Lazy Egg Sandwich Thing

This is a recipe that kinda tastes like and egg salad sandwich, but I don't really know what it is because idk how to make an egg salad sandwich.

  • 1 spoon

  • cereal (preferrably coco pebbles mmmmmmm)

  2. Grab box of cereal, and pour it in the bowl.
