Cereal

Cereal can be quite a challenge to make, but it's worth it when you soak your tongue with that milky goodness

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 2 bread
  • Potato Salad

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, and carefully place eggs in. Set a timer for 6 minutes, so they will be soft boiled.

  2. While the eggs are boiling, toast some bread.

  3. Put the toast on a plate, and cover one piece with potato salad. When the egg is done, unpeel it and slice it in half, placing the halves (runny) yolk down, and making a sandwich.
