Cereal

Cereal can be quite a challenge to make, but it's worth it when you soak your tongue with that milky goodness

Ingredients:

  • 1 spoon
  • 1 bowl
  • cereal (preferrably coco pebbles mmmmmmm)
  • milk (preferrably from a bull)

  1. Set bowl on table with spoon in it.

  2. Grab box of cereal, and pour it in the bowl.

  3. Grab your freshly harvested bull milk, and put it in the bowl.

  4. Commit mass homocide.
